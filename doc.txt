Donald John Trump is the Forty-fifth President of the country The United States of America. He is a candidate of the Republican party. He was preceded by Democrat candidate Barack Obama and will be succeeded by Democrat Joe Biden on the 20th of January 2021. Before entering into politics, he was a very renowned businessman and Television personality. He is often described as a populist, protectionist, purist and nationalist. He also became the oldest first-term President to be elected in the history of the United States. He won his presidential election in a surprise electoral college vote while losing the popular vote. He won against democratic Hillary Clinton.
